Frequently Asked Questions

What is Paffle and how does it work?

Paffle is a global financial platform that lets you bank, pay, and manage crypto across borders — all in one app. Sign up, get a virtual US bank account, and start sending or receiving money worldwide within minutes.

Who can use Paffle?

Paffle is built for freelancers, expats, exporters, and businesses that operate across borders. Whether you're receiving international payments or managing a global team, Paffle has you covered.

How do I get a virtual US bank account?

After signing up and completing identity verification, Paffle instantly assigns you a US routing and account number. You can share it with clients or platforms to receive USD payments just like a local.

What currencies does Paffle support?

Paffle supports a wide range of fiat currencies for transfers and payments, as well as major stablecoins and cryptocurrencies including USDT, USDC, and BTC. Real-time conversion is available at competitive rates.

Is Paffle available in my country?

Paffle is available in most countries worldwide. Visit our website or download the app to check availability in your region. We're continuously expanding our coverage to reach more global citizens.
